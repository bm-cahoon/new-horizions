Vision Statement

At New Horizons Tech Recovery, we believe in the transformative power of technology and education. Our vision is to empower individuals in recovery from substance abuse by providing them with the tools, skills, and certifications needed to forge a new path in the technology sector. We recognize that recovery is not just about overcoming a challenge; it's about building a new life. Our mission extends beyond the confines of traditional rehabilitation by investing in the future of those in treatment centers, offering a path to employment and a new life path.

We are looking to partner with recovery homes and 3/4 houses to offer accredited IT courses. Our goal is to offer hope and a concrete pathway to a fulfilling career, enabling our participants to not only reintegrate into society but to thrive as valued members of the tech community.

New Horizons Tech Recovery stands as a beacon of opportunity, illuminating a path to dignity, purpose, and economic independence. We are committed to changing lives, one certification at a time, by ensuring that our participants have access to quality education and the support they need to succeed. By investing in people at their most vulnerable, we create a ripple effect of positivity that extends far beyond the individual, fostering a culture of growth, resilience, and continuous improvement.

About

New Horizons Tech Recovery emerges from a place of profound personal understanding and triumph. Founded by a recovered addict who has navigated the road to renewal and achieved remarkable success in the technology sector, our mission is deeply rooted in the belief that recovery intertwines with the opportunity for professional rebirth.

We are looking to extend our support to those transitioning from treatment centers, providing a beacon of hope and a testament to what is possible. Our commitment is to invest in individuals when they are most in need, offering not just a second chance, but a credible and achievable future in the rapidly evolving tech landscape.

Our founder, now a Project Manager for a team of Site Reliability Engineers at NASA, stands as living proof that from the depths of addiction can rise a star of technological innovation and leadership. New Horizons Tech Recovery wants to embody this journey, offering accredited IT courses and certifications that serve as critical tools for re-entering society with valuable, in-demand skills.

How it Works

  • Find Your Certification: Choose any technical certification you're interested in pursuing. This could include certifications like CompTIA Security+, A+, Network+, or other relevant IT certifications that can help you transition into the technology sector.
  • Submit Your Story: Visit the New Horizons Tech Recovery website and fill out the submission form. Share your personal story, detailing your journey in recovery and your passion for entering the Information Technology field. Include specifics about the certification or training program you wish to undertake.
  • Review and Assistance: Once we receive your submission, our team at New Horizons Tech Recovery will review your application. We will strive to support your request by facilitating access to the course and certification test you selected. Please note that everyone is eligible for one class or certification test per year.
  • Funding and Support: All courses and tests are directly funded by New Horizons Tech Recovery through donations. We do not provide any direct financial assistance to individuals. Instead, funds go directly to cover the costs of the courses and certification tests.
  • Donation-Driven: New Horizons Tech Recovery is entirely supported by donations from individuals and organizations. We are not a rehabilitation center, but an organization focused on providing critical skills and training to those in recovery, empowering them to rebuild their lives by entering the Information Technology field.

How to Donate

At New Horizons Tech Recovery, your donations make a direct and lasting impact on the lives of individuals in recovery by providing them with the skills and certifications needed to build a new career in the technology field. Your generosity helps empower people to rise above their challenges and create a brighter future.

There are several ways to support our mission:

  • Online Donation: Visit our website and click the “Donate” button to make a secure contribution. You can choose a one-time donation or opt for monthly contributions to help sustain our efforts year-round.
  • Corporate Sponsorship: We welcome partnerships with businesses and organizations who are aligned with our mission. Your company’s sponsorship can help fund multiple certifications, making a powerful difference in the lives of many individuals.
  • In-Kind Donations: If you’re interested in contributing resources such as training materials, certification vouchers, or technology equipment, please get in touch. In-kind donations help lower operational costs, allowing more funds to go directly to the individuals we support.
  • Legacy Giving: Consider leaving a legacy by including New Horizons Tech Recovery in your will or estate plans. Your future gift can help us continue offering hope and opportunity to individuals for years to come.

Where Your Donations Go

While the primary focus of your donation is to fund IT training courses and certifications for individuals in recovery, a portion of contributions will go toward operational costs, including platform maintenance, outreach, advertising, and compensating staff. By investing in the infrastructure of New Horizons Tech Recovery, you are enabling us to reach more people and expand our impact, helping even more individuals achieve personal and professional success.
