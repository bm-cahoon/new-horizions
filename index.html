Vision Statement

At New Horizons Tech Recovery, we believe in the transformative power of technology and education. Our vision is to empower individuals in recovery from substance abuse by equipping them with the skills, tools, and certifications needed to forge new, meaningful paths in the technology sector. Recovery is not just about overcoming the past — it’s about rebuilding a life, and we’re committed to helping individuals do exactly that.

We partner with recovery homes, 3/4 houses, and other organizations to provide access to accredited IT certification programs and industry-recognized exams from leaders like CompTIA, AWS, Google, Cisco, and more. Through these efforts, we offer not just hope, but a tangible bridge to employment and self-sufficiency in the growing tech economy.

New Horizons Tech Recovery stands as a beacon of opportunity, dedicated to restoring dignity, purpose, and economic independence. One certification at a time, we are changing lives by ensuring access to quality education and meaningful support systems. By investing in people at their most vulnerable, we ignite a ripple effect of growth, resilience, and positive impact — reaching families, communities, and the tech industry itself.
