Vision Statement

At New Horizons Tech Recovery, we believe in the transformative power of technology and education. Our vision is to empower individuals in recovery from substance abuse by equipping them with the skills, tools, and certifications needed to forge new, meaningful paths in the technology sector. Recovery is not just about overcoming the past — it’s about rebuilding a life, and we’re committed to helping individuals do exactly that.

We partner with recovery homes, 3/4 houses, and other organizations to provide access to accredited IT certification programs and industry-recognized exams from leaders like CompTIA, AWS, Google, Cisco, and more. Through these efforts, we offer not just hope, but a tangible bridge to employment and self-sufficiency in the growing tech economy.

New Horizons Tech Recovery stands as a beacon of opportunity, dedicated to restoring dignity, purpose, and economic independence. One certification at a time, we are changing lives by ensuring access to quality education and meaningful support systems. By investing in people at their most vulnerable, we ignite a ripple effect of growth, resilience, and positive impact — reaching families, communities, and the tech industry itself.

About

New Horizons Tech Recovery is a nonprofit organization dedicated to providing opportunity, training, and support to individuals in recovery who are pursuing a career in technology. We are currently in the process of obtaining 501(c)(3) tax-exempt status, which will allow us to further our mission with greater transparency, eligibility for grants, and the ability to offer tax-deductible donations. Our goal is to create a sustainable and impactful organization that reinvests directly into the lives and futures of those we serve.

How it Works

Step 1: Find Your Certification

Choose any technical certification and training course that aligns with your career goals. This could include certifications such as CompTIA Security+, A+, Network+, or other relevant IT certifications designed to help you successfully transition into the technology sector.

Step 2: Submit Your Application

Visit the tab on the sidebar labeled Applicaiton and fill out the submission form. Share your personal story, detailing your journey in recovery and your passion for entering the Information Technology field. Include specifics about the certification or training program you wish to undertake.

Step 3: Review and Assistance

Upon receiving your submission, the New Horizons Tech Recovery team will review your application. Our goal is to support your request by funding the selected training course and certification exam on your behalf. If for any reason the chosen course and or exam lacks a voucher or external procurement option, we may recommend an alternative option.

Step 4: Funding and Support

All training courses and certification tests are directly funded by New Horizons Tech Recovery through donations. We do not provide any direct financial assistance to individuals. Instead, funds go towards the costs of the certification courses and exams.

Step 5: Donation-Driven

New Horizons Tech Recovery is entirely supported by donations from individuals and organizations. We are not a rehabilitation center, but an organization focused on providing critical skills and training to those in recovery, empowering them to rebuild their lives by entering the Information Technology field.

How to Donate

At New Horizons Tech Recovery, your donations make a direct and lasting impact on the lives of individuals in recovery by providing them with the skills and certifications needed to build a new career in the technology field. Your generosity helps empower people to rise above their challenges and create a brighter future.

Online Donation

Visit our website and click the “Donate” button to make a secure contribution. You can choose a one-time donation or opt for monthly contributions to help sustain our efforts year-round.

Corporate Sponsorship

We welcome partnerships with businesses and organizations aligned with our mission. Your company’s sponsorship can help fund multiple certifications, making a powerful difference in the lives of many individuals.

In-Kind Donations

If you’re interested in contributing resources such as training materials, certification vouchers, or technology equipment, please get in touch. In-kind donations help lower operational costs, allowing more funds to go directly to the individuals we support.

Where Your Donations Go

While the primary focus of your donation is to fund IT training courses and certifications for individuals in recovery, a portion of contributions will go toward operational costs, including platform maintenance, outreach, advertising, and compensating staff. By investing in the infrastructure of New Horizons Tech Recovery, you are enabling us to reach more people and expand our impact, helping even more individuals achieve personal and professional success.
